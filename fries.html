crispy, crunchy air fryer french fries are tricky, but this recipe guarantees perfectly amazing fries every time!

ingredients

  • 3 large potatoes
  • 2-3 tbsp olive oil
  • sea salt, pepper to taste

directions

  1. slice the potatoes into fries with a sharp knife and completely submerge them in a bowl of cool water.
  2. let them sit for about 1 hour to remove excess starch and help the fries crisp up more in the oven.
  3. drain the water and pat the fries dry with a paper towl.
  4. preheat the air fryer to 375Â°F.
  5. add fries to the bottom of the air fryer basket without stacking them on top of each other.
  6. cook for 15-20 mins until they are crispy and golden brown.
  7. place the fries on a cooling rack on top of a baking sheet lined with paper towels.
  8. let these fries sit in the oven on minimum temperature while the other batches of fries are cooking.
