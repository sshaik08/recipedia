this recipe will give you the crispest, sweetest chocolate chip cookies ever! inspired by the toll house chocolate chips, this recipe adjusts the original recipe by adding more brown sugar and less granulated sugar, as well as more vanilla. these cookies can be customized by adding nuts of your choice and can be stored in the fridge for 3-5 days or frozen for up to 3 months.

ingredients

  • 222g softened butter
  • 173g granulated sugar
  • 185g packed brown sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs
  • 342g all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt (if using salted butter) or 2 tsp salt (if using unsalted butter
  • 400g chocolate chips
  • optional: 120g chopped nuts

  2. cream the butter and sugars together in a large bowl with a stand mixer

  3. add the eggs one at a time and add the vanilla and mix well.

    • 2 eggs
  4. add the flour, baking soda and salt, mixing until a soft dough forms.
    • 342g all-purpose flour
    • 1 tsp baking soda
    • 1 tsp salt / 2 tsp salt (depending on which butter is used)s
  5. add in and mix the chocolate chips and nuts if used until evenly distributed
    • 400g chocolate chips
    • optional: 120g chopped nuts
  6. roll the cookie dough into balls (1 tbsp of dough each) and place on an ungreased cookie sheet
  7. bake the cookies for 7-9 minutes or until the edges are golden brown
