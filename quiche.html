crustless quiche is everything you know and love about classic quiches – minus the carbs! while you can put almost anything in it, this ultra easy meat-of-your-own and cheese crustless quiche takes less than 5 minutes to get in the oven!

ingredients

  • 100g diced meat of your choice
  • 1 sliced green onions
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • 5 large eggs
  • 1 cup cream
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper

directions

  1. preheat the oven to 350°F. grease a 9in pie dish with butter or spray lightly with oil.
  2. whisk the eggs, cream, salt and pepper.
  3. scatter most of the meat, cheese and green onion in the pie pan.
  4. pour in the egg mixture. top with the rest of the meat, cheese and green onion.
  5. bake 35 minutes until golden on top and centre barely wiggles. don't overcook!
  6. remove from the oven and let it rest for 5 minutes as it deflates.
  7. cut then serve with a simple side salad, or stuff into soft rolls, or wrap into burritos (then pan fry to crisp!)
