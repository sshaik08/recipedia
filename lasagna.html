Comfort Food

this delicious lasagna is perfect for feeding a crowd, sending a meal to a friend, and quick weeknight dinners!

ingredients

  • 1 lb lean ground beef
  • 1 (24-26 oz) jar marinara sauce
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 (12 oz) package no-boil, oven ready lasagna noodles
  • 1 (15 oz) container ricotta cheese
  • 2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

directions

  1. preheat the oven to 425Â°F and lightly spray an 11x7 in baking dish with nonstick cooking spray.
  2. brown the ground beef over medium-high heat and drain the excess grease away. bring the meat back to the skillet over medium-low heat and add marinara sauce and water. stir.
  3. layer about 1/4 of the meat sauce evenly in the baking dish. then add a layer of the noodles. add about half of the ricotta cheese and sprinkle about 1/3 of the mozzarella cheese.
  4. top with a layer of 1/4 sauce, a layer of pasta, the rest of the ricotta, and 1/3 more of the mozzarella.
  5. add another 1/4 of the sauce, another layer of pasta.
  6. top with the rest of the sauce and the mozzarella cheese.
  7. wrap the dish tightly with aluminum foil and bake for 1 hour or until the pasta is tender. allow to rest for about 10 mins before serving.
