make your mornings special with this easy french toast casserole. it has everything you love about traditional french toast, baked all in one pan! it's so good, you'll want it not only for breakfast, but also for lunch, dinner, and dessert too!

ingredients

  • 1/2 a loaf of bread, cut in cubes
  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 6 eggs
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp of cinnamon
  • brown sugar (topping, optional)
  • powdered sugar (topping, optional)

directions

  1. preheat oven to 425Â°F. butter a 13x9 inch baking pan and set it aside.
  2. in a small sauce pan, melt the butter over medium heat. once it's melted, stir in the brown sugar until dissolved and mixture is smooth.
  3. pour the mixture into the bottom of the pan and spread evenly.
  4. add the bread cubes to the pan, spreading them over the top of the brown sugar/butter mixture.
  5. in a medium sized bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, vanilla, and cinnamon.
  6. pour this mixture over the top of bread cubes, covering all the pieces of bread. sprinkle with cinnamon and brown sugar.
  7. bake in the oven for 25-30 mins. remove from oven and sprinkle with powdered/brown sugar if desired. enjoy!
