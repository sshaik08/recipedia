ingredients

  • 120g all-purpose flour
  • 175g granulated sugar
  • 44g dark cocoa powder
  • 4g baking soda
  • 2g baking powder
  • 3g salt
  • 120g buttermilk @ room temp
  • 118g coffee
  • 55g vegetable oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2g vanilla extract
  • 115g heavy whipping cream
  • 5oz dark chocolate chips

this ultra-moist chocolate cake is so easy to make, it can be called the lazy chocolate cake. we’re topping it with a simple chocolate ganache and enjoying this delight within 2 hours of taking out the ingredients!

directions

  1. preheat the oven to 325°F. prep an 8-inch round cake pan with cooking spray and parchment paper.
  2. mix the flour, sugar, cocoa, baking soda, baking powder, and salt in a bowl on low speed until combined.
  3. in another bowl, combine the buttermilk, coffee, oil, eggs, and vanilla.
  4. with the mixer on low speed, slowly add the wet ingredients to the dry.
  5. bake for 25-30 mins, or until a toothpick inserted in the center of the cake comes out clean.
  6. while it bakes: in a microwave safe bowl, heat the cream for about one minute. pour over the chocolate chips and stir until mixture is smooth. allow to cool slightly.
  7. cool in the pan for 10 mins, then turn them out onto a cooling rack and serve warm with the ganache
