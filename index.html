welcome to my recipedia!

here you'll find quick and tasty recipes for different kinds of food. whether you're craving something sweet, easy, or nostalgic, you'll find something to appeal to your tastes. these recipes are all simple enough but are still made to amaze your tastebuds! in addition, i've provided some tips and suggestions to look over before beginning your journey to satisfy your appetite. these guidelines will help you improve your efficiency in the kitchen and make sure that anything you create will exceed the expectations of even the most picky eaters! happy cooking!

stocking your pantry

a well stocked pantry is the halfway point to being an efficient cook. making sure your pantry contains staple ingredients will save you a trip to the grocery store last minute, especially when you want a quick meal. specific staple ingredients include:

  • olive oil
  • vegetable oil
  • butter
  • butter (salted and unsalted)
  • salt and pepper
  • garlic, onion powder
  • paprika
  • dried herbs
  • rice
  • pasta
  • flour
  • sugar

consider this a starting guide and not a comprehensive list of everything needed to whip up a feast in 20 minutes

cooking tips

when following a recipe, it's important to read through the entire thing (description, ingredients, directions) carefully to make sure that time-wise and ingredients-wise it is feasable. the last thing you want is to realize you're missing a key ingredient like the cocoa powder for your brownies. in addition, if you need to keep something in the oven/airfryer for 20 minutes when you only have 10 minutes to prepare your lunch for school, you'd want to realize sooner rather than later. lay out the ingredients and materials you need for a recipe before starting, so you can be sure that you have everything you need. as you begin cooking, taste your dish to make sure the spice/sugar/etc levels are to your liking. no recipe is perfect for everyone's tastes, and it's okay to stray a little from it. take notes of something being too much or not enough in your recipe, so you can adjust it next time. and finally, be patient. cooking is a science that can't be mastered in a day. take the time to let each step complete fully so that you can perfect and enjoy your dishes.
