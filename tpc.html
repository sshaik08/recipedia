ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 lb lean ground beef
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 2 bell peppers, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1.5 lb potatoes, diced
  • 1 oz taco seasoning
  • 1 1/2 cups salsa
  • 1 1/2 cips shredded cheese
  • toppings of choice

this taco potato casserole recipe is a great way to mix up taco tuesday with a flavorful dish the whole family will love!

directions

  1. preheat the oven to 400Â°F.
  2. heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. add ground beef and break it up with a spatula for 4-5mins.
  3. add in peppers, onion, garlic, and potatoes and stir. cover and cook for 10mins, stirring once halfway.
  4. remove the cover and cook for 10 more minutes stirring occasionally intil the potatoes begin to brown.
  5. turn off the heat and stir in seasoning, salsa, and 1 cup of cheese. transfer to a 9x9in baking dish and top with cheese.
  6. bake for 10 minutes and serve with toppings of choice.
