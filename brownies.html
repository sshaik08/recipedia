Brownies

Cake

Cookies

using basic pantry ingredients, and as easy as using a mix, this recipe makes brownies that are true perfection: glossy, fudgy, and filled with rich chocolate flavor!

ingredients

  • 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 2/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 3/4 tsp sea salt
  • 2 tbs milk
  • 1/2 cup canola oil or extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup dark chocolate chips

directions

  1. preheat the oven to 325Â°F. prep an 8-inch round cake pan with cooking spray and parchment paper. spray the parchment paper with cooking spray as well.
  2. medium size bowl: mix the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, powdered sugar, chocolate chips, and salt in a bowl until combined.
  3. large size bowl: whisk the eggs, olive oil, milk, and vanilla in a bowl until combined.
  4. sprinkle dry ingredients over the wet ingredients as you stir until combined.
  5. pour the batter into the pan, using a spatula to smooth the top.
  6. bake for 40-48 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean. note: it's better to pull the brownies out early than to leave them in too long!
  7. cool completely before slicing
