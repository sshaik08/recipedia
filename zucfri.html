this homemade zucchini fritters recipe is so easy to make and gets you perfect fritters every time! crispy on the edges, tender in the middle, and never soggy, these fritters are loved by all ages!

ingredients

  • 2 lb zucchini (2 large/5 medium)
  • 1 1/2 tsp fine sea salt, divided
  • 2 large eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1/2 cup chopped green onions
  • 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • sour cream, for serving

directions

  1. wash and grate the zucchini, then place in a large bowl and stir in a teaspoon of salt.
  2. after 10 mins squeeze out the water and transfer the zucchini (about 3 1/2 to 4 cups) in a mixing bowl.
  3. add 1/2 cups chopped green onion and 2 lightly beaten eggs.
  4. in a small bowl, stir together the flour, baking powder, black pepper, and the rest of the salt.
  5. add the flour mixture into zucchini mixture and stir until well combined.
  6. place a large heavy-bottomed cast iron or non-stick skillet over medium heat and add olive oil.
  7. add the zucchini mixture in heaping tablespoons one at a time and flatten out the tops slightly to make them pancake-shaped.
  8. sauté for 3-5 minutes per side or until golden brown. serve warm with a dollop of sour cream.
